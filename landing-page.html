Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lumen really free?
Yes! 100% free. No trials, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. The AI runs locally on your Mac using open-source models.
How is this different from other reading trackers?
Lumen combines emotion tracking, AI-powered insights, and smart organization. Plus, everything runs locally - no cloud dependencies, no API costs, total privacy.
What about privacy?
Your data NEVER leaves your Mac. All AI processing happens locally using Ollama. No cloud sync, no analytics, no tracking. You own your data completely.
Do I need to install Ollama?
No! Lumen works great without AI. You'll still get algorithmic insights, emotion tracking, collections, and reading queue. Ollama is optional for AI-powered summaries.
Which Macs are supported?
macOS 12+ (Monterey and newer). Both Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) and Intel Macs supported. For AI features, 8GB+ RAM recommended.
Can I contribute or request features?
Yes! Lumen is actively developed. Reach out on LinkedIn or GitHub with feature requests and feedback.
